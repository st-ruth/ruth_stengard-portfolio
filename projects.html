Electric Banana Land

My first website was a prep course assignment to create a fictional zoo with hover info boxes. Inspired by Electric Banana Band - I designed Electric Banana Land!

Cultural painter

Let me introduce another version of myself - the building conservator! In 2023 I trained as a cultural painter and learned everything about older painting techniques and paint. With this education I have gained knowledge about stencil painting, adhesive paint, everything you will ever need to know about linseed oil paint, marbling, graining, etc.

Digital illustrations

When I get some time to myself, I like to spend it drawing, preferably on my iPad. I've found it to be a fun and forgiving way to experiment and work in layers. I made all of these creations on Procreate - a tool I'm still learning more about every time I use it, and one that I think will come in handy in my future career!
